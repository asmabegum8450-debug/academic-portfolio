About Me

Hello! My name is Asma Begum and I am a Computer Science student at the University of Washington in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering.

I strive to direct my passion for technology and innovation toward building equitable, inclusive solutions that make an impact.

Beyond academics, I am actively involved in the Computer Science Club and Women in STEM, where I engage in collaboration, leadership, and community building. My goal is to pursue a career in software engineering while continuing to learn, innovate, and contribute to inclusive tech communities.
